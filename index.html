About LTS

Little Taco Shop (LTS) was founded with one mission: to bring authentic, mouthwatering Mexican street tacos to our community. Our recipes are inspired by traditional flavors from Mexico City and Oaxaca. Every taco is made with love, using fresh ingredients and homemade tortillas.

We started as a food truck. Today, we have a cozy location serving hundreds of happy customers daily. Our commitment to flavor and freshness keeps people coming back for more!

When did Tacos first appear in United States?

Tacos first appeared in the United States around 1905, introduced by Mexican migrants working on railroads and in mines.
